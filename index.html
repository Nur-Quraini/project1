About the project

Hi there! Linguaverse: Dutchman's Ship Chapter is an interactive English courseware designed for Semester 3 students at UiTM Shah Alam. By using Unity as the development engine, we manage to create an immersive learning courseware for them. It focuses on grammar, reading, listening, and pronunciation through lessons, quizzes, and assessments that shape the story based on your performance. The better you do, the more your adventure unfolds! What began as a class assignment has since earned multiple gold awards in competitions. I had a fantastic time collaborating with my team and receiving invaluable guidance from our advisor, Mr. Haziq. Be sure to check us out!
